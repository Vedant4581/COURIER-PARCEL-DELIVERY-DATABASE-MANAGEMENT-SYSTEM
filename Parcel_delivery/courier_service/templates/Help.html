Shipping Guidelines

1. Packaging Instructions:

  • Use the right-sized box, use appropriate padding materials (e.g., bubble wrap, packing peanuts), and seal the package with strong tape.
  • We encourage customers to double-check that the contents are secure and won't shift during transport<

    • 2.Labeling and Addressing:

  • Use legible and waterproof labels. Printed labels are suggestable over handwritten.
  • Stress the importance of accurate recipient information, including the correct street address, postal/ZIP code, and contact number.
  • We recommend adding a return address label to facilitate the return of undeliverable packages.

    • 3.Tracking Number Security:

  • We Advise customers to keep their tracking numbers confidential and only share them with trusted parties.
  • There may be chances of accessing your order details if you share tracking numbers publicly, such as unauthorized access to shipment details.

    • 4.Shipping Restrictions:

  • Food Items that no longer lasts over the delivery duration are strictly prohibited.
  • Liquid items that can leak out of the package are not allowed.
  • Any explosives, inflammable items are not allowed.
